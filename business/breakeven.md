## Unit Economics & Break-even Analysis for ipv-guard

### Cost per Active User
- **Compute Costs**: $0.05 per hour (average usage of 720 hours/month)  
  - Monthly Cost: $0.05 * 720 = **$36.00**

- **Storage Costs**: $0.02 per GB (average usage of 10 GB per user)  
  - Monthly Cost: $0.02 * 10 = **$0.20**

- **Bandwidth Costs**: $0.01 per GB (average usage of 100 GB per user)  
  - Monthly Cost: $0.01 * 100 = **$1.00**

**Total Cost per Active User**:  
$36.00 (Compute) + $0.20 (Storage) + $1.00 (Bandwidth) = **$37.20**

### Pricing Tiers
1. **Basic Tier**: $50/month  
   - Features: Basic IPv6 prefix management, 1 user, 10 GB storage, 100 GB bandwidth.

2. **Pro Tier**: $100/month  
   - Features: Advanced IPv6 prefix management, 5 users, 50 GB storage, 500 GB bandwidth, priority support.

3. **Enterprise Tier**: $250/month  
   - Features: Custom IPv6 prefix management, unlimited users, 200 GB storage, 2 TB bandwidth, dedicated support.

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Range
- Estimated CAC: **$150 - $300** per user (including marketing, sales, and onboarding costs).

### Lifetime Value (LTV) Estimate
- Average customer lifespan: 24 months  
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU): $100/month (assuming a mix of tiers)  
- LTV = ARPU * Customer Lifespan = $100 * 24 = **$2,400**

### Break-even Users Count
- Monthly Cost per User: **$37.20**  
- Monthly Revenue per User (Average): **$100**  
- Break-even Point = Total Fixed Costs / (Revenue per User - Cost per User)  
- Assuming fixed costs of $1,000/month:  
  - Break-even Users = $1,000 / ($100 - $37.20) = $1,000 / $62.80 ≈ **15.95 users** (rounded to **16 users**)

### Path to $10K MRR
- Target Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR): **$10,000**  
- Average Revenue per User (ARPU): **$100**  
- Required Users = $10,000 / $100 = **100 users**

#### Tier Distribution for $10K MRR
- **Basic Tier**: 40 users = $2,000  
- **Pro Tier**: 30 users = $3,000  
- **Enterprise Tier**: 30 users = $7,500  

Total: $2,000 + $3,000 + $7,500 = **$12,500** (exceeds target, adjust as needed)

### Summary
To achieve $10K MRR, we can target a mix of user tiers, with a focus on acquiring at least **100 active users** across different pricing tiers. The break-even point is approximately **16 users**, allowing for a clear path to profitability.
